about me

My name is Bram Van Echelpoel

I'm 21 years old and live in Antwerp, Belgium. Currently, i'm studying graphic and digital media at the AP College in Antwerp.

In my free time,

photography is my biggest passion. Mainly travel photography, but when i'm home i also like to do some lifestyle and experimental photography. I'm also a scout leader at the Scouts from Halle-Zoersel.

My first "professional" graphic design project

was designing posters, flyers, tickets, wristbands,.. for a big event in my area. It's called "Het Grote bosgebeuren", a big festival-like party in a forest with 3 stages and mainly electronic music from famous artists, several of them who also played at Tomorrowland. It has about 3000 visitors each year.
